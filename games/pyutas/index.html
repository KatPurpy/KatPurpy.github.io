Pyutas




Description

PyuTas is a game about pew pew(lazers) and insane shootouts with lazers.
You are the computer user who tries to clear his local network from the computer virus. Use your only weapon(lasers) to vaporize the infection.

Good luck!

Features

  • 4 levels of difficulty from braindead to rip!
  • 6 challenging levels!
  • Responsible controls!
  • Clear retro arcade styled graphics!
  • Custom game engine based on MonoGame!
